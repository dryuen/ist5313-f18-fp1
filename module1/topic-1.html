Welcome to The Course

Introduction to FoodWeb Course ~~~~~~~ Introduction to Module 1

Hello! Thank you for accessing What Every Beginner Chef Needs to Learn. This course is designed to give the beginner chef the information needed to set up a kitchen and create basic meals.

The course has three modules.

  • Module 1: Basic Cooking Equipment
  • Module 2: Meal Preparation and Food Safety
  • Module 3: Intro to Baking

Course Structure

This is an independent learning course. This means that you, as the learner can visit any page you want in any order. Take your time and view each page, including the option content. Please enjoy the YouTube videos and links to external online content that will add to your knowledge of the topics. The quiz will open when you have visited all of the pages in the module. Once you have successfully mastered the quiz, a certificate of completion will be available for you to print. Please enjoy the course.

Module 1

Module 1 will introduce participants to kitchen equipment. Once familiar with the basic tools, the beginner chef can safely begin to create satisfying dishes.

Objectives

The purpose of this module is to familiarize the novice chef with simple kitchen tools and equipment.

Topic Objective
Measurement Recognize the purpose and proper use of measurement tools.
Knives Learn knife purposes and handling techniques.
Pans Determine the correct pan for the job.
Activity Practice learned skills.
Quiz Test acquired knowledge.
