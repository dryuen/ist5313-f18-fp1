Welcome to The Course

Hello! Thank you for accessing What Every Beginner Chef Needs to Learn. This course is set up to give the beginner chef the information needed to set up a kitchen and create basic meals.

The course has three modules.

  • Module 1: Baking Cooking Equipment
  • Module 2: Meal Preparation and Food Safety
  • Module 3: Baking

Module 1

Module 1 will introduce participants to kitchen equipment. Once familiar with the basic tools, the beginner chef can safely begin to create satisfying dishes.

Module 1 Objectives

The purpose of this module is to familiarize the novice chef with simple kitchen tools and equipment

Topic Objective
Measurement Recognize the purpose and proper use of measurement tools.
Knives Learn knife purposes and handling techniques
Pans Determine the correct pan for the job
Activity Practice learned skills
Quiz Test acquired knowledge
