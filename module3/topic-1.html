Welcome to Module 3

Baking… Is it a science or an art? Many people say both, however, there are so many factors that go into baking with how ingredients react.! Temperature is a big factor- Do you use butter that is cold from the fridge, room-temperature, or melted? Depends on what you’re making.

Baking can be considered home chemistry. If you add the ingerdients incorrectly the experiment fails. In this module the participant will learn the basic tools that bakers use and the formulas and calculations necessary for success.

Beginner chefs will familiarize themselves with the equipment and processes that professional bakers use. Look for tips to make your recipes more successful.
