These tools make baking a breeze!

Kitchenaid Mixer

Having a Kitchenaid mixer is helpful for quickly mixing ingredients. Plus, they are easy to clean, and you can purchase accessories to simplify your baking tasks!

Scale

Using a scale makes baking a lot more accurate. Flour, when measured in weight will give you better results than when measured by volume.

Cookie Scoop

Many bakers use cookie scoops, or ice cream scoops, to make perfectly round cookies! You scoop them out from the batter, then release on to the cookie sheet!

Cooling Rack

Placing cooked foods on these wire racks allow cooling from all sides. The stainless steel wire pans are closely aligned mesh squares that allow air to reach the bottom of food and keeps it from getting soggy.

Rolling Pin

Long and cylindrical, rolling pins are used to roll out dough. The pin can also be used as a crushing tool. Rolling pins are often made of marble, porcelain, wood, and glass. They can be solid cylinders or have a pin down the center connected to the handles.
