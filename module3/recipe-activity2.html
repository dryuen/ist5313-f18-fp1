Build Your Own Cookie

Selections

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups butter or margarine, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups Gold Medal™ all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF. Mix butter, sugar, vanilla and eggs in a large bowl using a spoon. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Then, stir in chocolate chips.
  2. Drop dough by rounded measuring tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  3. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes or until light brown. Cool slightly. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack; cool.
