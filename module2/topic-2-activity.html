Wash your hands for 20 seconds before and after you prepare each meal.

Steam is one of the most common causes of kitchen burns.

Wash countertops, cutting boards, and other surfaces after preparing foods, especially after handling raw meat.

Having raw chicken and vegetables on the same cutting board spreads bacteria.

Food in the refigerator should be separated and covered to keep it fresh.

Knives should always be set flat on surfaces with blades pointed inward. This blade is upward and out.

Use a thermometer to check that meat is prepared to the correct internal temperature.

Dirty hands can spread bacteria.

Ensure that foods are stored at the appropriate temperatures.
