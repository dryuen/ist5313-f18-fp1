Recipe Activity

Build your own Stir Fry

Cook meat in a frying pan until it is the correct internal temperature. Cook starch until al dente. Saute vegetables until they are the desired consistency. Have diners choose the items they want in their dish and throw the starch in a heated pan. Add the vegetables and meat and toss with the desired sauce until warm. Serve in a bowl.
