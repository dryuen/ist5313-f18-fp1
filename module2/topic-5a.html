Slower Cooker Breakfast

French Toast

A delicious recipe that can be prepped the night before!

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf French bread, cubed
  • 6 egg
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1-1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, divided
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup brown sugar, packed,
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg

Prep-Time-4 1/2 hours , Cook-time-2 to 4 hours

Directions

  1. Place bread cubes in a large bowl. In a separate bowl, beat together eggs, milk, vanilla, and ½ teaspoon of cinnamon
  2. Pour egg mixture over bread; mix well. Cover and refrigerate overnight for at least 4 hours.
  3. Spoon bread mixture into a lightly greased 6 quart slow cooker.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together butter, brown sugar, remaining cinnamon, and nutmeg; sprinkle on bread mixture.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or high for 2 hours. Let stand 15-20 minutes before serving.

Easy to Reheat Breakfast Dish

Mini Breakfast Pies

A recipe that can be made ahead of time and reheated quickly on busy mornings.

Ingredients:

  • ¾ pound of breakfast sausage
  • ⅛ cup of minced onion
  • ⅛ cup of minced green bell pepper
  • 1 can (12 ounces) of refrigerated biscuit dough
  • 3 eggs-beaten
  • 3 tablespoons of milk
  • ½ cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese

Prep-Time-20 min , Cook-time-20 min

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. In a large, deep skillet over medium heat, combine onion, sausage, and green pepper. Cook until the sausage is evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
  3. Separate dough into 10 individual biscuits. Flatten the dough out, then line the bottom and sides of 10 muffin cups.
  4. Evenly distribute sausage mixture between the cups.
  5. Mix together the eggs and milk, then divide between the cups.
  6. Sprinkle top with shredded cheese.
  7. Bake in oven for 18-20 minutes.

Nicole’s Favorite Crock Pot Meal:

Honey BBQ Slow Cooker Chicken Wings -- This recipe is perfect for game night, and what’s great about preparing it in a slow cooker is the fact that you can multitask while it’s cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsps salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsps garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 4 lbs chicken wings
For the sauce
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • ½ cup honey
  • 3 tbsps apple cider

Cooking and Prep Time: 2 hours + 15 minutes

Directions

  1. Pat dry the chicken wings before placing them in the slow cooker.
  2. Add the dry ingredients, ensuring that the wings are evenly coated.
  3. Combine ingredients for the sauce with a whisk in a small mixing bowl.
  4. Pour the mixture into the slow cooker and toss wings with a fork to ensure an even coating.
  5. Cook in the slow cooker on HIGH for 2 hours (with the lid on, of course!).
  6. Garnish with parsley.
  7. Serve with celery sticks and/or ranch dressing.

Brogan’s 15 Minute Meal:

Pizza Rolls--This is a quick meal that everyone in the family will love! :)

Ingredients:

  • Crescent rolls
  • Mozzarella string cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Pizza sauce

Time-15 minutes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. On cooking sheet, roll out crescent rolls. On each roll, put 3-4 pepperoni slices. Cut string cheese into halves. Place one half of string cheese on top of pepperoni. Roll crescents.
  3. Bake in oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve with pizza sauce and enjoy!

Wendy's Family Casserole

Enchilada Casserole

A family meal you can prepare ahead of time and quickly place in the oven to heat when ready to eat.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 can of red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • Corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Prep-Time- 25 min, Cook-time- 20

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown ground beef in pan
  3. While browning beef, combine enchilada sauce, cream of chicken, and cream of mushroom in a pot and heat
  4. In a 9x13 pan break corn tortillas into pieces, enough to cover bottom of pan
  5. Add a layer of ground beef, then a layer of the soup/enchilada sauce mix. Then top with shredded cheese
  6. Add a second layer of all ingredients. Then slightly combine
  7. Add a top layer of shredded cheese
  8. Bake in oven for about 20 mins or until cheese is melted on top

Ashly’s Favorite Pressure Cooker/Instant Pot Meal:

Dal Tadka Lentil Recipe-- Cheap, delicious, and incredibly healthy. Lentils are a great go to for a quick dinner and an irresistible comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • 9oz chana dal (yellow)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 small red onion chopped
  • 3-4 whole green chillies
  • ¾ ginger root
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 tomatoes
  • ¾ tsp ground turmeric
  • ¾ tsp garam masala
  • 1 ½ tsp ground coriander
  • fresh coriander for garnish
  • salt to taste

Prep time- 20 minutes Cook time- 30 minutes

Directions

  1. In a pressure cooker add 3 tbsp of vegetable oil and set heat to medium.
  2. When the oil is ready add the cumin seeds and allow to pop and cook for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add the onion, chillies, ginger and garlic. Stirring frequently for about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add the turmeric, garam masala, and ground coriander. Stir for about 1 minute until spices become fragrant.
  5. Add the tomatoes and stir for about 8 minutes until the mixture has turned into a paste.
  6. Add in the chana dal and stir, mixing all of the ingredients together and adding salt to taste.
  7. Cover the mixture with two cups of water and close the pressure cooker.
  8. Allow the mixture to cook for about 4 whistles on a medium heat. After the 4th whistle turn off the stove and let the lentils cool down naturally.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and enjoy!

Chris' Favorite Meal:

Restaurant Style Parmesan Crusted Chicken-- If you love this popular dish from BJ's Restaurant, now you can prepare it from the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 chicken breasts,
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1⁄2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Japanese-style bread crumbs (panko)
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Cook Time- 45 minutes

Directions:

  1. Make brine for chicken by dissolving salt with chicken broth in a medium bowl.
  2. Cover chicken breasts with plastic wrap and pound each one to about 1/2 inch thick with a kitchen mallet.
  3. Add flattened chicken to brine. Cover and chill for 3 hours.
  4. When chicken has marinated, remove the fillets from brine and dab with paper towels to remove excess liquid.
  5. Beat eggs in a medium bowl, and pour flour onto a plate. Combine breadcrumbs, parmesan cheese, salt and pepper in another medium bowl.
  6. To bread the chicken, first coat each fillet with flour, then egg, and then the Parmesan mixture.
  7. Add enough oil to cover the bottom of a large saute pan, and heat over medium/low until hot.
  8. Saute each breaded chicken fillet in oil for 4-5 minutes per side or until brown.
  9. Remove fillets to a paper towel-covered plate until ready to serve.

Robecca's Updated Classic 😅

Adult Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • prepackaged deluxe macaroni and cheese
  • grilled chicken strips (frozen or fresh)
  • bacon bits
  • 3 tablespoons sour cream
  • 4 cup package of mixed shredded cheese

Prep Time-20-30 min, Cook Time 10-15 min

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Prepare macaroni and cheese to package directions
  3. Grill chicken strips until they reach a cooked temperature of 165 degree
  4. Mix cheese sauce into cooked noodle. Add sour cream, bacon, and chicken strips.
  5. Take macaroni mixture and spoon 1/3 into a 9 x 13 glass baking pan
  6. Add a layer of cheese and extra bacon
  7. Add half of the remaining macaroni mixture to the pan
  8. Add another layer of bacon and cheese
  9. Add the last of the macaroni mixture and top with cheese and bacon
  10. Cook in oven for 20 to 25 minutes (until cheese is melted inside)
