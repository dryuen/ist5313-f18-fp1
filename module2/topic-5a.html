Pizza Rolls--This is a quick meal that everyone in the family will love! :)

Ingredients

Crescent rolls, Mozzarella string cheese, pepperoni slices, pizza sauce.

Time-15 minutes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  3. On cooking sheet, roll out crescent rolls. On each roll, put 3-4 pepperoni slices. Cut string cheese into halves. Place one half of string cheese on top of pepperoni. Roll crescents.
  4. Bake in oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve with pizza sauce and enjoy!
