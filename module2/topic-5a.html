Pizza Rolls--This is a quick meal that everyone in the family will love! :)

Ingredients:

  • Crescent rolls
  • Mozzarella string cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Pizza sauce

Time-15 minutes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. On cooking sheet, roll out crescent rolls. On each roll, put 3-4 pepperoni slices. Cut string cheese into halves. Place one half of string cheese on top of pepperoni. Roll crescents.
  3. Bake in oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve with pizza sauce and enjoy!

      Dal Tadka Lentil Recipe-- Cheap, delicious, and incredibly healthy. Lentils are a great go to for a quick dinner and an irresistible comfort food.

      Ingredients:

      • 9oz chana dal (yellow)
      • 2 cups of water
      • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
      • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
      • 1 small red onion chopped
      • 3-4 whole green chillies
      • ¬æ ginger root
      • 3 cloves garlic
      • 3 tomatoes
      • ¬æ tsp ground turmeric
      • ¬æ tsp garam masala
      • 1 ¬Ω tsp ground coriander
      • fresh coriander for garnish
      • salt to taste

      Prep time- 20 minutes Cook time- 30 minutes

      Directions

      1. In a pressure cooker add 3 tbsp of vegetable oil and set heat to medium.
      2. When the oil is ready add the cumin seeds and allow to pop and cook for about 2 minutes.
      3. Add the onion, chillies, ginger and garlic. Stirring frequently for about 3-5 minutes.
      4. Add the turmeric, garam masala, and ground coriander. Stir for about 1 minute until spices become fragrant.
      5. Add the tomatoes and stir for about 8 minutes until the mixture has turned into a paste.
      6. Add in the chana dal and stir, mixing all of the ingredients together and adding salt to taste.
      7. Cover the mixture with two cups of water and close the pressure cooker.
      8. Allow the mixture to cook for about 4 whistles on a medium heat. After the 4th whistle turn off the stove and let the lentils cool down naturally.
      9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and enjoy!
