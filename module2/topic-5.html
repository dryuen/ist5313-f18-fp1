Read Twice Prep Once

ALWAYS MAKE SURE TO READ THE WHOLE RECIPE BEFORE COOKING- TWICE!
This is important to do for many reasons.

  1. There may be ingredients to be used that are not in the ingredient list (Ex: water).
  2. You may need a specific tool, pot, or pan.
  3. There may be a technique for cutting, or cooking that you have not done before. (Ex: Do onions need to be chopped or diced?
  4. Ingredients may need to be split for different uses. (Ex: ½ cup butter for one part, then ½ for another?
  5. The oven may need to pre preheated.

Once you have read the entire recipe, make sure to gather all of the equipment, then gather all ingredients. Make sure to follow the recipe step by step. If you are new to cooking, it is best to not use substitutions.
