Welcome to Module 2

Have you ever wanted to start dinner only to look around your kitchen and get discouraged? Perhapes the area was not ready for cooking. Maybe the recipe seems too difficult. You begin to question yourself.

  • What should I prepare?
  • Do I have everything I need?
  • Will I be able to complete this task?
  • How do I keep my family healthy?

All of these questions can be answered in this module. Participants will learn how to keep the kitchen a safe healthy place. Beginner chefs will discover the benefits of meal planning and how best to shop for groceries.
