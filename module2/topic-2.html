Safe Meat Tempuratures

Measure the tempurature of these Meats prior to serving

Catagory Food Temperature (℉)
Ground Meats Beef, Pork, Veal, Lamb 160
Fresh Beef, Veal, Lamb Steaks, Roasts, Chops 145
Poultry Chicken or Turkey;whole 165
Breasts, Roasts 165
Thighs, Legs, Wings 165
Duck and Goose 165
Stuffing ( alone or inside of bird)
Pork or Ham Fresh Pork 145
Fresh Ham ( Raw) 145
Precooked Ham(Reheat) 140

Keep this in Mind!

Make sure your meat is the correct temperature before serving.

Cooking meat to the correct temparature is only one step to keeping your kitchen safe. Visit the follwing website for more information.
