Benefits of Meal Planning

If you ask someone why they meal plan, you will probably get one of the following three answers:

  1. Time

    Having meals planned and even prepared beforehand cuts back on time in the kitchen, as well as trying to decide the night of what to cook.

  2. Money

    This is a big one. With meal planning, you figure out your meals, then create a grocery list based on your meals. When shopping, ONLY get those groceries. Plus, if you have a plan ready, you are less likely to eat out...as that gets expensive!

  3. Nutrition

    Planning meals ahead of time helps you to make sure you have the healthy foods you need. Plus, as said before, you will eat out less. Eating out can be very unhealthy!

There are several types of meal planning. Depending on your time and lifestyle, choosing one or different ones through your week may be helpful.

Frozen Meals (Prep before and then bake)

This is useful for when you don't wish to prepare meals throughout the week, as well as for prepping many meals at once. You can easily have 5 meals prepped in a few hours time for under $100.

Quick 15 minute dinners

Having a few quick, simple recipes as go-tos is a great way to put dinners together.
